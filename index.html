ATOMIC RIFFS BLOWS UP

"Emerged from the desolation landscape of a massive nuke explosion..."

Somewhere in the middle of nowhere, were once exist one of the most advanced civilization, now decay over the pass of time, the radiation of the fallout continue… Under the waste of a collapse skyscraper, deep down the surface, there is a basement, a bomb shelter… From the outside you can hear the scratches, the fuzz, the bumble hiss, waves of noise crashing and smashing with each other… it’s very unstable!
It's known by now that this nuked sound it’s generated by some sort of mutant species living between the dirt, the old tube amplifiers and the magnetic death tapes! We call them... ATOMIC RIFFS!!
